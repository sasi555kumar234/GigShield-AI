# GigShield-AI - Income Protection for Delivery Partners

## 📌 Problem Statement
Delivery partners (Swiggy/Zomato) lose 20–30% of their income due to:
- Heavy rain
- Extreme heat
- App downtime
- Curfews

Currently, there is no system to compensate for this income loss.

---

## 💡 Solution
GigShield-AI is an AI-powered parametric insurance platform that:
- Tracks delivery activity
- Monitors external disruptions
- Automatically triggers payouts for lost income

---

## 👤 Target Users
Food Delivery Partners

Example:
Ravi (24, Delivery Worker)
- Earns ₹600–₹900/day
- Income drops during bad weather
- Needs financial protection

---

## 🔄 Workflow
1. User selects a weekly plan
2. System tracks activity
3. Monitors external disruptions
4. AI predicts expected income
5. If disruption occurs:
   - Claim triggered automatically
   - Instant payout processed

---

| Plan | Price | Coverage |

## 🔑 Parametric Triggers
- Rainfall > 50mm
- Temperature > 42°C
- AQI > 300
- App downtime > 2 hours
